Verdict
Overall finding

NotebookLM won on research rigour. Gamma won on presentation quality. The optimal workflow was to use Claude for initial synthesis, NotebookLM for fact-checking and audio generation, and Gamma for final visual production. The most significant output, however, was produced by none of the three designated tools — the 3D Human Evolution Explorer represents a fourth category of research output: an explorable experience rather than a viewable presentation.
